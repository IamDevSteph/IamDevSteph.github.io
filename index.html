Greetings

I am a full stack developer ready to bring my skillset to the stage. Before I began my journey in coding I took an interest in coding in 2021 from watching youtube videos of various programmers in their select channels talk about how fulfilled they are in their careers. It ignited a spark of interest in myself to learn more about the world of technology. I realized that programmers are able to create fascinating intelligence from their fingertips to their keyboard. Creativity is a skill I thoroughly enjoyed but never thought as to where and how I can implement my vision. Through my company website I found there was an educational program that encourages associates currently working at Amazon to enroll in these programs whether it is staying inside the Amazon network or exploring other opportunities at a collaborating employer. After learning about this resource I made a personal goal to make it to my 1 year anniversary which is the requirement at the time to enroll in the Career Choice program. I noticed a web/software developer coding bootcamp called We Can Code IT (WCCI) and decided to take charge and upskill my abilities to become a full stack software developer!
